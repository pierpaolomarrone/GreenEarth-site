ABOUT US
We are a Startup that deals with environmental impact projects such as reforestation and ocean cleaning, our goal is respect for mother nature.
We are collaborating with famous companies such as "TREEDOM", "OCEAN CLEANUP" and "PLASTIC FREE".
OUR PROJECTS
TREEDOM PARTNERSHIP
Since 2020 we have created a partnership with the large Treedom company, thanks to which we have managed to replant 1 million trees between Brazil, Central Africa and Argentina.
THE OCEAN CLEANUP
We captured our first plastic in 2019 and in 2021 we reached proven technology.Today, our total catch runs to hundreds of tons and counting, and as we continuously improve our operations,our catches become larger and more reliable.The fundamental challenge of cleaning up the ocean garbage patches is that the plastic pollution is highly diluted, spanning millions of square kilometers. Our cleanup solution is designed to first concentrate the plastic, allowing us to effectively collect and remove vast quantities..
PLASTIC FREE
We are an Italian Free Onlus, together with over 260,000 volunteers we have organized more than 6,500 environmental clean-up appointments, giving new life to territories submerged by plastic and waste.
